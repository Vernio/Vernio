Hello my name is Paweł Wernio

I’m UX Designer focused on building valuable products for humans.

;

Helloo!

I’m happy to see you!

I love to shape products to increase value for the users. Solving usability problems, reducing pain points, analyzing user behaviors and needs, building hypotheses about product improvements and check all of that by validating tests is what's occupied me for the day. I’m a big enthusiast of the customer development approach, to reflect the product team's hypothesis and see how users react to them. Design thinking is a set of methods I’m using to ideate possible solutions. Making so I’m involving a whole team. Cooperative work creates great outcomes. Aligning the product team and give the space for each member to contribute is the best way to work. I love this holistic approach. After research and ideation phases, I like to focus on creating easy user flows where intuitive interface helps accomplish quickly planned objectives. To make it happen a validation test with users or A/B testing is what I do to bring data and facts to the table and support the decision process. The outcome of that is value delivered to customers with every initiative. What’s gives the whole team best satisfaction at the end of the day. Base on my experience within a product team I can organize product teamwork, grooming backlog, prioritize tasks and be a helpful info share-point when it comes to contact with stakeholders and business.
