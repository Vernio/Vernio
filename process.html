User research techniques like exploratory interviews, diary studies, desktop competitive research, personas creations are crucial to identify user problems and needs. At this stage the goal is to define findings and narrow them to the most important insights ready for the next step - solutions ideation.

Backed up with solid data and facts about users there is a crucial part to bring all product team to the ideation workshop. Aiming for the best ideas with all constraints in mind. Conducting vivid cooperation with the goal to produce user flows, mocks, clickable prototypes and product hypothesis.
